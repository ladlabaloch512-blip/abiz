How long does it take to see results from digital marketing?

Results vary depending on the strategy. SEO typically takes 3-6 months for organic results, while PPC can show results within days. Social media results can be seen within 1-3 months.

What is the difference between SEO and PPC?

SEO focuses on improving organic rankings for long-term results. PPC involves paid advertising for immediate traffic and visibility.

Why is local SEO important for my business?

Local SEO helps your business appear in local searches, attracting nearby customers and driving foot traffic to physical locations.
